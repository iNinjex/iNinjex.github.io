SEARS

A pioneer brand that reshaped the retail landscape.

How did a simple watch company grow into America's favorite store in the 20th century? Let's get into the retail time machine and journey together to the past.

1886

The company was founded by Richard Warren Sears in the year 1886.

1908

Sears began selling entire home kits through mail-order catalogs.

1925

Sears opened its first retail store on Chicago's west side.

1974

As a towering symbol of retail dominance, Sears unveiled the Sears Tower. It was world’s tallest building for 22 years.
