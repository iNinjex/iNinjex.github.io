Website Redesign Case Study

Problem

The original website had usability issues such as confusing navigation and poor layout, making it difficult for users to find information.

Process

I analyzed the existing design and identified key areas for improvement, including navigation, layout, and visual hierarchy.

Design Decisions

I simplified the navigation, improved spacing, and used clearer visual structure to guide users.

Testing

Feedback showed improved usability, and adjustments were made based on that feedback.

Final Product

The final design is cleaner, easier to navigate, and provides a better user experience.
