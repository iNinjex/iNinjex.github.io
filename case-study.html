Sears Website Redesign Case Study

Problem

The original experience felt difficult to navigate because users needed a clearer way to browse categories, search for products, and move through the shopping process.

My Role

This was a team project, but I focused on front-end layout, navigation, and usability improvements across pages like Home, Search, Product, Cart, and Help.

Process

I analyzed the existing structure and improved navigation, spacing, visual hierarchy, and consistency across pages.

Design Decisions

Navigation was made consistent, layouts were cleaned up, and UI elements were styled to improve clarity and usability.

Testing and Feedback

I tested navigation, responsiveness, and layout across pages. Feedback helped improve spacing, linking, and mobile usability.

Final Product

The final site provides a cleaner layout, better navigation, and a more complete shopping experience.
